Congrats to [Jianhan Zhang](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jianhan-zhang-175536231/) who was awarded Highest Honors by the Statistics Undergraduate Curriculum Committee for his honor thesis work "Counterfactually Fair Offline Reinforcement Learning via Sequential Marginal Distribution Mapping."

This work also won `Inaugural Director’s Award for Distinguished Honors Thesis` (“Best Thesis Award”).
